Advanced Frequently Asked Questions - Solar Solutions

1. Solar panel lagwane ka process kya hai?

Site survey, design, installation, commissioning aur testing ke steps follow kiye jate hain. Solar panel lagwane ka process: 1️⃣ Survey & Planning – Location aur load analysis. 2️⃣ Subsidy & Documentation – Eligibility check aur apply. 3️⃣ Installation – Panel, inverter aur wiring setup. 4️⃣ Net Metering – Bijli vibhag se approval (On-grid ke liye). 5️⃣ Activation & Handover – System start karna aur guidance dena. 🔥 Free electricity + Subsidy ka fayda! Contact karein 📞

2. Kya solar panels rainy season me effective hote hain?

Haan, solar panels rainy season me bhi kaam karte hain, lekin efficiency 20-50% tak kam ho sakti hai due to clouds & rain. Fir bhi, diffused sunlight se power generate hoti hai, aur net metering ya battery backup se bijli ki problem nahi hoti. ⚡🌦

3. Government subsidy kaise apply karein?

Gouri Enterprises & Solar aapke liye solar water pump aur rooftop solar par subsidy lene me poori madad karega. ✅ Hum aapko sarkari yojnao ki jaankari, documents ki madad aur apply karne ki process sikhayenge, taki aap kam kharche me apna solar system lagwa sakein. 🌞⚡ 🚜 Kisano ke liye – Solar water pump subsidy se bijli ka bill khatam aur sichai asaan! 🏡 Ghar ke liye – Rooftop solar subsidy se bijli free aur kamayi bhi ho sakti hai! Agar aap bhi solar system lagwana chahte hain aur subsidy lena chahte hain, to humse turant sampark karein! 📞💡

4. Solar panel maintenance kaise karein?

Regular cleaning, inspection aur performance checks se panels ki efficiency barkarar rakhi jati hai. 1️⃣ Har 1-2 mahine me saaf karein (dhool-mitti hatayein). 2️⃣ Shadow check karein (ped/building ki chhaya na ho). 3️⃣ Wiring & inverter inspect karein (6 mahine me ek baar). 4️⃣ Net meter & generation monitor karein. 5️⃣ 1-2 saal me expert se full check-up karwayein. ✅ Proper maintenance se zyada power aur long life milegi! ⚡🌞

5. Kitne kW ka solar system mere liye sahi hai?

Aapke energy consumption, available space aur budget ke hisab se customized system design kiya jata hai.And Aapke liye solar system ka size aapke daily electricity consumption par depend karta hai. 1 kW system 3-4 units per day produce karta hai. Agar aapki monthly consumption 300-400 units hai, to 3-4 kW ka system sahi rahega. Aapko exact size ke liye survey karwana hoga.

6. Installation me kitna time lagta hai?

Typically 1 se 3 din, site ke size aur configuration par depend karta hai.Solar panel installation me 2-3 din lagte hain, lekin net metering approval aur documentation process me 10-15 din tak lag sakte hain.

7. Kya solar panels ka lifespan hota hai?

Haan, aam taur par 25-30 saal tak chal sakte hain.Haan, solar panels ka lifespan 25-30 saal hota hai. Uske baad bhi panels kam efficiency se kaam karte hain, lekin proper maintenance se unka life aur badh sakta hai.

13. Solar panels ke kitne types hote hain?

Monocrystalline, polycrystalline, aur thin-film panels commonly use hote hain. 1️⃣ Monocrystalline – High efficiency, long lifespan. 2️⃣ Polycrystalline – Slightly less efficient, cost-effective. 3️⃣ Thin-film – Lightweight, flexible, but less efficient.

14. Aapke solar panels ki efficiency kitni hai?

Efficiency typically 15-20% ke beech hoti hai, lekin (monocrystalline, polycrystalline) model ke hisab se vary kar sakti hai.

17. Grid-tied system kya hai aur kaise kaam karta hai?

Grid-tied solar system ek aisa solar power system hai jo directly electricity grid se connected hota hai. Ye system battery storage ke bina kaam karta hai aur generate ki gayi extra electricity grid me feed karta hai.
### Kaise Kaam Karta Hai?
1. Solar Panels – Suraj ki roshni se electricity generate karte hain (DC power).
2. Inverter – DC power ko AC power me convert karta hai, jo ghar ya business ke liye usable hoti hai.
3. Electricity Usage – Pehle solar energy use hoti hai, agar demand zyada ho to grid se power li jati hai.
4. Net Metering – Agar extra electricity generate hoti hai to ye grid me feed hoti hai, aur iska credit bill me adjust ho jata hai.
Ye system low maintenance, cost-effective aur government subsidy ke saath aata hai, jo ise popular banata hai.

18. Off-grid solar system kaise operate hota hai?

Off-grid systems batteries me store ki gayi energy ka use karte hain aur grid se disconnected rehte hain. - Solar Panels: Suraj ki roshni se DC power generate karte hain.
- Charge Controller: Battery ko safe charging aur overcharge se protect karta hai.
- Battery Bank: Generated power ko store karta hai.
- Inverter: Stored DC power ko AC power me convert karke appliances ko supply karta hai.
Is tarah, yeh system grid se bilkul independent hai.

19. Solar panels ko kitni der clean rakhna chahiye?

Regular cleaning, har 6 mahine se 1 saal me, performance maintain karne ke liye zaroori hai. Solar panels ko hamesha clean rakhna chahiye taki unki efficiency bani rahe. Aam taur pe:
- Har 6 mahine me cleaning kafi hoti hai.
- Agar area dusty ya polluted hai, to 3 mahine me bhi cleaning kar sakte hain.
Isse panel par jama dust aur grime door rehta hai aur maximum performance milti hai.
